When upload-based mergers are a poor fit

Avoid uploading files that include legal agreements, private IDs, financial records, HR paperwork, medical details, or client documents covered by confidentiality rules.

For those jobs, a free PDF merger no sign up that runs locally in your browser is usually a better fit.
